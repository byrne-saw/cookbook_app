Fava Bean side dish

Chef: Joe

Prep Time: 2 Hours, 5 Minutes

Ingredients:

  • ice
  • tap water
  • 2 lbs fresh fava beans in the pod
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves

Directions:

  1. boil 4 cups of water
  2. add salt
  3. place fava beans in the boiling water for 5 min
  4. remove fava beans and place in ice water
  5. add seasoning to taste
